Welcome to Damodar Water Park

Situated along the Coastline of Danda-Kelve Creek (Danda Khadi) and a walkable distance from Kelve Beach on the outskirts of Kelve Fishermen Village is our water park that features water play areas, such as colorful water slides, wave pool, swimming pool, and barefooting environment with delicious food and foot-tapping music.

Damodar Water Park offers you 1 Big Wave Pool, 2 Swimming Pools, 4 Thrilling Water Slides, Eye Catching Environment, Tasty Food, Luxury Rooms, Locker Rooms, Own Parking Facility.

Our Services

Are you looking for a quiet, relaxing and peaceful family vacation spot? Then you are at right place Damodar Waterpark, Kelwa provides you the fresh and healthy environment that nourishes your body and soul. At just 8 km away from Kelwa Road railway station Damodar Waterpark situated at Kelwa, Palghar.

Damodar Waterpark is an ideal place within the Kelwa for great holiday to spend with your family and friends. The sound of laughter and splashing water in our park creates an energetic atmosphere. Our amusement park offers a fantastic experience for all ages. Children have all the fun as if there are no tomorrows, whereas grown-ups re-create their Childhood memories and desires by enjoying with laughter and fun at our water park. We create happy memories, one slide at a time.
